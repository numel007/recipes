# Lime Crema

## Ingredients

| Name          | Quantity |
| ------------- | -------- |
| Sour cream    | 8 oz     |
| Lime zest     | 1 tsp    |
| Lime juice    | 2 tbsp   |
| Salt          | 0.5 tsp  |
| Garlic powder | 0.5 tsp  |

_Yield: ~1 cup_

## Method

1. Combine all `ingredients` and chill for 1 hour before serving.

### _Notes & Comments_

- Excellent as a dip for quesadillas.
